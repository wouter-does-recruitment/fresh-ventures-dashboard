Biggest drop-off points
⚠️ Biggest drop — Typeform stage
57% of candidates who completed the typeform did not finish the video interview. This is the single biggest bottleneck in the process.
⚠️ High drop — Sign-up to Typeform
48% of applicants never completed the typeform after signing up. A follow-up nudge or simplified form could recover significant volume here.
📋 Review to Interview — 33% drop
A third of candidates who were ready for review didn't make it to interview. This likely reflects deliberate filtering — but worth checking if any strong profiles were missed.
💛 Offer acceptance — 69%
25 of 36 offers were accepted (69%). 11 declined — understanding their reasons could help improve the offer stage for the next cohort.
✅ Strong — Video to Review
Only 7% dropped between video completion and ready-for-review. Once candidates finish the video, they are highly committed to the process.
